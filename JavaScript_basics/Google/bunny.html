Bunny Facts


Here are some interesting facts about bunnies


  • Bunnies are born with their eyes closed and without fur.
  • They can rotate their ears almost 180 degrees to detect sound.
  • Bunnies are social animals and enjoy the company of other rabbits.
  • They have a lifespan of around 10 years.
  • Bunnies are herbivores and love to eat hay, vegetables, and fruits.
  • Their teeth never stop growing, so they need to chew on things constantly.
